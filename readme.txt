Add highlights and other text effects to put emphasis on your writing.

== Description ==

Catch the eye and draw attention to your words with distinct styles. Realistic "hand-drawn" highlighter effects and other formatting methods.

You are welcome to add comments and questions into the Plugin Support page. I will be as active as possible with responses.

== Installation ==

1. Upload and activate the 'Stand Out' plugin.
1. You can add [standout] tags around any text. Don't forget to close them. [/standout]
1. Alternatively, you can use a drop down menu in the text editor to pick your effects.
1. Your words will be emphasized in your posts.
